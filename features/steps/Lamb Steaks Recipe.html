Lamb Steak Recipe


Ingredients

  1. 4 center-cut lamb steaks (approx. 3 lbs., can substitute beef ribeye)
  2. 1 tsp. kosher salt
  3. 1 tsp. ground black pepper
  4. 1 tsp. garlic powder
  5. 1 tsp. onion powder (granulated onion)
  6. 4 tsp. fresh thyme, minced
  7. 1 tbsp. olive oil

Instruction

  1. STEP 1. Mix your choice of marinade and pour it over the lamb steaks. Let them absorb flavors for a few hours, covered and refrigerated.
  2. STEP 2. Heat the grill to high or alternatively heat a grilling pan or cast iron skillet over medium-high heat. Lightly oil the surface of the grill or pan.
  3. STEP 3. Pat dry the lamb steaks and discard the marinade. Season them with salt and pepper. Place them on the grill over direct heat.
  4. STEP 4. Place the cooked steaks on a platter and let them rest for 5 minutes. You can tent them loosely with foil if youâ€™d like to keep them warm.
